Chicken Kebab

The deliciousness of Chicken Kebab lies in its succulent, tender chunks of marinated chicken, expertly seasoned and grilled to perfection. Each bite offers a harmonious blend of flavors, with the marinade infusing the meat with a rich, aromatic taste that tantalizes the palate. The charred edges add a delightful smokiness, enhancing the overall depth of flavor. Chicken Kebab is not only a culinary delight but also a versatile dish, pairing well with a variety of accompaniments such as fresh salads, warm bread, or flavorful sauces. Its irresistible aroma and mouthwatering taste make it a favorite among food enthusiasts, offering a delightful experience that satisfies both the senses and the soul.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into cubes
  • Bell peppers, cut into chunks
  • Red onion, cut into chunks
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic, minced
  • Greek yogurt
  • Paprika
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper
  • Skewers

Steps

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a bowl, combine Greek yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, paprika, ground cumin, ground coriander, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Add the cubed chicken to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results, to allow the flavors to meld.
  3. Prepare the Skewers: If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the skewers, alternating with bell peppers, red onion chunks, and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the Kebabs: Place the assembled kebabs on the preheated grill. Cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are charred and tender.
  6. Serve: Remove the kebabs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. Serve hot with your favorite sides and enjoy!
